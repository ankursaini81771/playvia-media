PlayVia Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

At PlayVia, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Secure email address, username, and password collected using Supabase Authentication.
  • Media Files: Video clips and thumbnail images uploaded by creators. Thumbnails are stored in Supabase Storage, and video assets are delivered via secure CDN paths.
  • Usage Data: Likes, comments, and view counts used to feed personalized metrics to users and advertisers.

2. Device Permissions

PlayVia requests access to the following native device permissions to enable streaming and upload actions:

  • Camera: To capture video clips directly within the app.
  • Microphone: To record clear audio along with your camera video clips.
  • Photo Library / Storage: To select pre-recorded videos and thumbnails from your gallery.

3. How We Use Information

We use your data to provide video streaming, handle premium subscriptions, enable community comments, and display advertiser campaign metrics on the dashboard.
